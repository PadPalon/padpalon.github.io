Surely it hasn't been three months since the last post.

Surely we haven't entered a whole new year already.

Well, at least I have plenty of videos to share with you!

<!--more-->

# [*Suno, AI Music, and the Bad Future*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U8dcFhF0Dlk) by Adam Neely

Yeah, it's more AI videos, bite me, this shit sucks so bad. This is the first video I've watched that hits extra close to home,
since it's about GenAI in music, a topic that is VERY important to me. Neely is a great musician and video essayist, and manages
to to break the whole thing down in a way that makes it clear where he stands, with enough openness that someone that likes AI
might watch enough to actually learn something.

This video gets first billing simply for a procrastination-shattering transition when the video moves into the politics of it all.
You'll know it when you hear it. Literally made me boot up my editor and start putting this together. Inspiration through released
frustration via medium of shouting or something.

# [*they want you to stop.*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CtzSHVh0mgw) by Jacadamia

GenAI makes me swing wildly between being depressed about the state of the world and being angry at the state of the world. This video
deserves a shoutout because it takes those feeling, and tries to refocus the viewer on their own creativity. Shit sucks, but we can't
let them keep us down. This is something I struggle heavily with, so I really appreciate content like this. I even turned on my synths
for a bit this week!

# [*White Supremacy in Metal*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2CybKNI8kbU) by Brutus Bathory and Myke C-Town

Time for a change in topic, surely we'll find something more light-hearted.

:(

Completely new creator for me, but unfortunately, a topic I am all to familiar with. I've been in the metal scene for almost two decades now
(oh god), been to countless concerts, from tiny local shows to huge festivals, and organized local shows for several years. While it has always
baffled me, it is an unfortunate reality that there are bigots and fascists in our midst, and the thing of "Metalheads look evil, but are
the nicest people!" has given cover to those people by being taken as "Metalheads CAN'T be evil".

Don't want to go on a whole tirade, but I. Hate. These. Assholes! I'm a cishet white dude and attract these dudes (let's be real, it's
mostly dudes), they feel reeeaaal comfortable around me, so I've heard some shit! And people wonder why we have a diversity problem in the scene ...

# [*Disney's Living Characters: A Broken Promise*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NyIgV84fudM) by Defunctland

A 4-hour video about animatronics-meets-performer thingies? Talk about emotional whiplash after those other videos!

Defunctland consistently puts out great videos with excellent production quality, so this kinda feels like a cheat entry, but let me tell you,
if you've never watched his stuff because the topics don't interest you before watching, just put one on and suddenly 4 hours have passed.
Ask me how I know.

# [*The Psychological Horror Game That Messed Us All Up - Mouthwashing*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HuD-ypEKP4o) by PushingUpRoses

First video on Mouthwashing I've watched, and it's by one of my favourite creators from way back when I first started watching stuff on the internet.
Roses is a super cool person, and I was so happy to have this video pop up for me.

# [*the value of being analog + how to get off your phone*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sp1LVFA4ueg) by silver fox

This video speaks to me, because I long for that offline connection, to art especially, but haven't found my own balance yet. I also absolutely
need my background noise, and thought organization systems, and memory helpers, so I'll probably never land on the fully offline end of things.
Worth it as a motivational goal though.
